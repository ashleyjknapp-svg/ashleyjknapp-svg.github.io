AI & Operations

Building marketing that actually scales.

The real differentiator isn’t the campaign — it’s the infrastructure behind it. Here’s how I’ve used AI, automation, and systems thinking to solve real problems and drive measurable outcomes.

01

Monday.com Campaign Management Platform

The problem

Campaign and creative production were managed through disconnected tools and email threads — no visibility, no process enforcement, and no capacity tracking across a 15-person marketing organization.

What I built

Led full implementation of Monday.com as the single source of truth for the entire marketing org. Built the board architecture, workflow automations, status categories, and integrations from scratch. Trained the team and embedded the platform into campaign planning, creative production, agency management, and field requests.

~30% output increase · 15-person org · 14+ SOPs built · Full team adoption
02

“Oil Changes for Good” — End-to-End Fundraising Automation

The problem

Leadership challenged marketing and operations to build a community fundraising program from scratch. No vendor, no template, no playbook — just a brief and a deadline.

What I built

Designed the full program model, built a branded intake system where nonprofits apply, get reviewed for eligibility, and automatically receive a custom marketing package — flyer, email, and social post pre-filled with their organization name, store locations, dates, and unique coupon codes. Coordinated AP, IT, and Operations for end-to-end execution. Fully automated.

43 nonprofits enrolled · 16+ states · $2,926 donated · Zero manual fulfillment
03

Automated Promotional Asset Delivery

The problem

Updating promotional flyers and pricing menus for 300+ locations took two full weeks of a designer’s time every quarter — across 17 brand versions and 7+ document types. Sometimes we outsourced it entirely.

What I built

Partnered with IT to build PDF form templates connected through Power Automate and Encodian. Center managers select their store from a dropdown, choose their materials — flyers, nametags, Google review sheets, pricing menus — and receive everything in their inbox within seconds, pre-populated and optimized for printing.

2 wks → sec 300+ locations served · Outsourcing eliminated · Power Automate + Encodian
04

On-Demand Discount Code System

The problem

Corporate field managers needed fast recovery tools for unhappy customers, but uncontrolled discount access creates abuse and zero accountability — a real tension in any franchise operation.

What I built

Built a bank of one-time-use coupon codes in the POS system spanning multiple discount tiers, connected through a Microsoft Form with automated routing. Field managers submit a request with store, reason, and discount needed — and receive an unused code immediately, with a full audit trail for accountability.

Real-time code delivery · Full audit trail · POS + Microsoft Forms · Replicable for franchise
05

Budget Actualization & Invoice Automation

The problem

Invoice processing was manual and disconnected, with no store-level attribution — making it impossible to trace marketing spend to individual locations across 300+ stores, multiple brand pools, and a corporate/franchise split. Leadership had limited visibility into what was being spent where and why.

What I built

Designed a standardized invoice coding system with store-level attribution across all business units (ROP, franchise NMF/SAF, ADI). Built a full chart of accounts, processing SOPs, and a structured monthly close cadence — accruals, stakeholder review, finance reconciliation, and executive reporting on planned vs. actual. The model was adopted for franchise materials fund forecasting and allocation. Currently automating the full pipeline: invoice arrives in inbox → auto-coded, saved to SharePoint, forwarded to AP — no manual steps.

3 wks faster vendor payments · 10+ min/invoice eliminated · Franchise fund adoption · Exec-level spend visibility
